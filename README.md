# Shipeng (Henry) Liu - Personal Academic Website

This is the personal academic website of Shipeng (Henry) Liu, a Ph.D. student in RoboLAND at the University of Southern California (USC). The website showcases research projects, publications, and academic background.

## 🎯 About

This website serves as a comprehensive portfolio for academic and research activities, featuring:

- **Personal Information**: Academic background, research interests, and contact details
- **Research Projects**: Detailed pages for ongoing and completed research projects
- **Publications**: Academic papers and publications
- **Media**: Videos, presentations, and research demonstrations
- **CV/Resume**: Professional experience and qualifications

## 🔬 Research Focus

The website highlights research in:
- **Human-Robot Teaming**: Collaborative decision-making algorithms
- **Adaptive Embodied Agents**: Intelligent robotic systems
- **Embodied Active Sensing**: Sensing strategies during locomotion
- **Multi-Robot Cooperation**: Coordinated robotic systems
